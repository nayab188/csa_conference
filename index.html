Overview

The Department of Computer Science and Applications at KLEF successfully hosted a two day National Conference on Multidisciplinary Research in Computer Science & Applications. This academic event brought together over 204 participants from academia, research, and industry to explore emerging trends, interdisciplinary collaborations, and innovations in areas such as:
  • Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning
  • Data Science
  • Cybersecurity
  • Cloud Computing
  • Smart Systems & IoT

Session Highlights & Impact

Technical Sessions

The conference featured multiple parallel technical sessions, covering a wide spectrum of topics. Some notable paper presentations included:

  • Smart Traffic Control Using AI
  • Facial Emotion Recognition using Deep Learning
  • Blockchain-Enabled Voting Systems
  • Cloud Security and Storage Models
  • AI in Agriculture and Healthcare Applications
